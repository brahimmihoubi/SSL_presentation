SDG Skills Lab

Learn. Build. Impact.

About the Event

"SDG Skills Lab" is a dynamic, multi-week educational initiative designed to equip students with practical, high-demand skills through hands-on training. In collaboration with Algérie Télécom and with production support from Rdjem Studio, the series will run over several weeks — each dedicated to a different theme. This program reflects the mission of the Skill Center to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world application.

Key Information

  • Launch Date: August 15, 2025
  • Location: Skell Center, Sétif
  • Format: Weekly in-depth workshops
  • Audience: University students

Organizers & Partners

  • Organized by: Setif Developers Group (SDG)
  • Main Partner: Algérie Télécom
  • Production Partner: Rdjem Studio
  • Partners: Nasda, GDG

Program

The program is structured around a weekly theme, providing deep dives into critical skill areas. Each week concludes with a mini-project and certification.

General Format

  • 1 or 2 Themes in 1 Week
  • 5 Days of Workshops
  • 4 hours of training daily
  • Final Day: Mini project & Q&A

Each Workshop Includes

  • Real use cases
  • Tools walkthrough
  • Group projects or tasks
  • Certification per theme

Conclusion

SDG Skill Lab is not just about training — it's about building a generation of students ready to lead, create, and adapt in a changing digital world. By combining technical skills, entrepreneurship, language mastery, and soft skills, this program empowers students to become valuable contributors.

With strong partnerships, this initiative is backed by real expertise. Students will walk away with skills, certifications, and confidence to move forward in their careers.
