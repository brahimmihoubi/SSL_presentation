About the Event

The "SDG Skills Lab" is a dynamic, multi-week educational initiative aimed at equipping participants with practical, high-demand skills through hands-on training. This program is designed not only for students but also for professionals, educators, and community members seeking to enhance their skill sets in today's competitive landscape.In collaboration with Algérie Télécom and with production support from Rdjem Studio, the series will unfold over several weeks, with each session dedicated to a different theme relevant to various sectors. This initiative reflects the mission of the Skill Center to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and real-world application, fostering a collaborative environment where individuals from diverse backgrounds can learn, share, and grow together.By participating in the "SDG Skills Lab," you will gain valuable insights, network with industry experts, and develop the competencies necessary to thrive in an evolving job market.

Program

The program is structured around a weekly theme, providing deep dives into critical skill areas. Each week concludes with a mini-project and certification.

General Format

  • 1 or 2 Themes in 1 Week (11 Themes)
  • Between 2-5 Days of Workshops
  • 4 hours of training daily
  • Final Day: Mini project & Q&A

Each Workshop Includes

  • Real use cases
  • Tools walkthrough
  • Group projects or tasks
  • Certification per theme

Conclusion

SDG Skill Lab is not just about training — it's about building a generation of students ready to lead, create, and adapt in a changing digital world. By combining technical skills, entrepreneurship, language mastery, and soft skills, this program empowers students to become valuable contributors.
